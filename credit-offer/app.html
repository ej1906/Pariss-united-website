Your Action Plan

                        
Follow these instructions precisely to ensure your legal rights are protected.

                        
    
                            
  • 1. Review and Complete: Carefully read each letter. Fill in your personal information (Name, Address, DOB, SSN) at the top.
    • 
                            
  • 2. Print: Print each of the three letters on separate sheets of paper.
    • 
                            
  • 3. Sign: Physically sign your name in the space above your typed name on all three letters.
    • 
                            
  • 4. Prepare Enclosures: Make clear photocopies of your driver's license (or other state-issued ID) and a recent utility bill. Include a set of these copies with *each* letter.
    • 
                            
  • 5. Mail Separately: Take the three separate envelopes to a USPS Post Office. Send each letter using Certified Mail with Return Receipt Requested. This is non-negotiable and creates your legal paper trail.
    • 
                            
  • 6. Wait and Track: The bureaus have approximately 30 days to respond. Keep your certified mail receipts and the green return receipt cards when they arrive back in your mail.
    • 
                            
  • 7. Escalate if Necessary: If a bureau fails to respond or gives an inadequate response, immediately file a formal complaint against that bureau with the Consumer Financial Protection Bureau (CFPB) at www.consumerfinance.gov.
